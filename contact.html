Atomic Design System

Save your designers & developers up to 50% of their time with a custom-built, object-driven Design System for your product.

Style Guide

A foundation which defines your brand typography, colors, icons, spacing and information architecture. The foundation proved essential for guiding work in a unified direction while allowing room for you to individually explore creative design solutions.

Components

Consider components as elements of a living organism. They have a function and personality, are defined by a set of properties, can co-exists with others and can evolve independently.

Library

Organized individual components into artboards containing similar items. These artboards are then organized by general category. The library is referred to throughout the design and development process.
